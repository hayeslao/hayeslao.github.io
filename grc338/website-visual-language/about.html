Our Story

Eien Ramen was founded in San Luis Obispo in February 2026. It started off as a humble ramen shop and grew into a hotspot for late night meals. The Japanese word "eien" (or "永遠") means eternity. We aim to serve meals that put everlasting smiles on the faces of those who eat them. Our housemade noodles, fresh ingredients, and carefully-crafted broth ensure that each slurp brings you closer to "eternity."

Next, Eien Ramen plans on expanding its horizons into other noodle dishes, while maintaining its pristine, homemade quality that has brought customers joy for so many years.
