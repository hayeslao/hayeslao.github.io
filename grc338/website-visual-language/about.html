Our Story

Eien Ramen was founded in San Luis Obispo in 1982. It started off as a humble ramen shop with three employees in the kitchen and serving customers. For years, our noodles have remained beloved for their addicting taste and consistent quality. Recently, we expanded our menu to include our unique spins on other classic Japanese noodle dishes. Now, in 2026, Eien Ramen has grown into a local hotspot for late night meals in Downtown SLO.

The Japanese word “eien” (or “永遠”) means “eternity” or “forever.” Eien Ramen takes its name from the desire to serve delicious noodles forever. We aim to serve meals that put everlasting smiles on the faces of those who eat them. Our housemade noodles, fresh ingredients, and carefully-crafted broth ensure that each slurp brings you closer to eternity.

Next, Eien Ramen plans on expanding its horizons into other highly-requested noodle dishes, while maintaining its pristine, homemade quality that has brought customers joy for so many years.
